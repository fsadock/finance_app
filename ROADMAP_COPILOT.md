# Copilot Money Feature Roadmap

This document outlines the planned features to bring this application to parity with **Copilot Money**, focusing on high-fidelity visualizations, AI automation, and proactive financial insights.

## Phase 1: Dashboard & Visual Insights
*   **"Free to Spend" Metric**: A dynamic calculation on the dashboard showing remaining discretionary income after bills, subscriptions, and budget commitments.
*   **Spending Pace Graph**: Overlay an "ideal" spending line (dotted) vs. actual spending (solid) on the main monthly chart to track pacing.
*   **Sankey Cash Flow**: An interactive diagram showing the flow of money from Income -> Fixed Bills -> Discretionary Categories -> Savings.
*   **Net Worth History**: A dedicated chart tracking assets vs. debts over time (6mo, 1yr, All).

## Phase 2: Advanced Budgeting (Adaptive Logic)
*   **Rollover Budgets**: Option to carry over unused budget from the previous month to the current one.
*   **Smart Rebalancing**: A tool to suggest moving funds from under-spent categories (e.g., "Transport") to over-spent ones (e.g., "Food") to stay within the total monthly limit.
*   **Custom Tags**: Support for cross-category tracking (e.g., #trip2026, #wedding) with dedicated reports.

## Phase 3: Investment & Performance
*   **Portfolio Benchmarking**: Compare your investment performance against standard indices (S&P 500, IBOVESPA).
*   **Allocation Diversity**: Improved donuts for asset class (Stocks, Bonds, Crypto) and sector allocation.
*   **Dividend Tracker**: Automated detection and projection of dividend income.

## Phase 4: AI & Natural Language
*   **Natural Language Search**: A "Copilot Chat" or search bar where users can ask "How much did I spend at Amazon last year?" or "Show me my largest restaurant bills."
*   **Monthly Recap "Stories"**: A visual, story-style summary at the end of each month highlighting spending trends and net worth changes.
*   **Price Increase Alerts**: AI alerts when a subscription (detected in Recurrings) increases in price.

## Phase 5: Ecosystem & Automation
*   **Amazon/Venmo Itemization**: Deep integration to fetch line-items for large aggregate transactions (if supported by Pluggy/manual upload).
*   **Manual Accounts**: Support for tracking manual assets like Real Estate or Vehicles with manual price updates.
*   **Push Notifications**: Alerts for large transactions, low balances, or reaching 80% of a budget limit.
